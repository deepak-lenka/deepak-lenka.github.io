I am a Founding AI Engineer and Researcher at Birla AI Labs.

I am a college dropout currently building Birla AI Labs at the Aditya Birla Group, contributing to the Structured Data Time-Series Foundation Model (TSFM), India's first foundation model for time-series data. My work bridges applied research and product engineering, turning complex models into real, usable products.

Before that, I contributed to open-source projects, including chat-llm by OpenAI researcher Gabriel Petersson. It is a power-user UI for ChatGPT that runs 100% locally, supports split-screen workflows, lets people ask multiple questions at the same time, and opens saved chat configs with one click.

I have contributed to many more projects too. Open source has been one of my main ways of learning and building cool stuff.
